About Me

Hi, I'm Marina! I'm a frontend developer who is passionate about creating digital products that are both beautiful and easy to use. My philosophy is simple: technology should help, not complicate. I love turning complex problems into elegant interfaces that people enjoy using again and again.

To improve my English, I decided to mix business with pleasure, so I'm currently working as a barista to immerse myself in the language. This experience has given me great soft skills, but I'm ready to move forward and fully dedicate myself to a career in IT.

Outside of work, my life is filled with walks with my corgi and my passion for photography. I'm very curious and always learning something new—from the latest JavaScript frameworks to the secrets of the perfect filter coffee. I believe that constant development is the key to success and enjoying life.
